Humpack Whales

They have the longest migration on the planet.

  • Scientific Name: Megaptera novaeangliae
  • Average lenght: 12 to 16 metres
  • Average Lifespan: 45 to 100 years
  • Habitat: Oceans

While humpback bodies are mainly black and grey in color, the white patterns on their pectoral fins, bellies, and the undersides of their tails differ between individuals. Tails (or flukes) can grow to be 18 feet wide and are serrated along the edge with a sharp pointed tip.
